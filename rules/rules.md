# Introduction

These rules apply to all communities, groups, supergroups, channels, voice chats, events, bots, services, and platforms operating under the authority of the TSB Council unless explicitly stated otherwise.

By participating in any TSB community, you agree to comply with these rules.

These rules exist to maintain a safe, active, enjoyable, and welcoming environment for everyone.

Use common sense. Respect others. Respect boundaries.

---

# Rule 1 — Respect & Community Conduct

Treat all members with basic respect and decency.

Friendly banter, roasting, teasing, memes, and casual humor are generally permitted.

However, the following are prohibited:

* Targeted harassment
* Bullying
* Intimidation
* Threats
* Stalking
* Hate speech
* Discrimination
* Repeated personal attacks
* Deliberately making members feel unsafe

Members are expected to respect personal boundaries.

Agar kisi member ne clearly request kiya hai ki aap kisi specific behavior ko stop karein, that request should be respected.

The administration and Council may intervene whenever behavior becomes harmful to individuals or the community.

---

# Rule 2 — Community Disruption

Members must not intentionally create disruption within the community.

Examples include:

* Creating unnecessary drama
* Deliberately provoking fights
* Encouraging conflict between members
* Sabotaging community activities
* Attempting to divide the community
* Repeatedly disrupting normal operations

Disagreements are natural.

Creating chaos is not.

---

# Rule 3 — Spam & Flooding

Active participation is encouraged.

Spam and flooding are prohibited.

Examples include:

* Excessive message flooding
* Repeated copy-pasted messages
* Excessive emoji spam
* Sticker spam
* GIF spam
* Artificial activity inflation
* One-word spam chains
* Deliberately splitting messages to flood chat

During approved events, celebrations, competitions, game nights, VC parties, and milestones, moderation teams may temporarily relax spam restrictions.

Deliberate abuse of chat activity systems may result in moderation action.

---

# Rule 4 — NSFW Content

TSB communities are not NSFW communities.

The following are prohibited:

* Pornographic content
* Explicit images
* Explicit videos
* Explicit GIFs
* Explicit stickers
* NSFW links
* Sexually graphic content

Mature humor, double-meaning jokes, and light adult humor may be tolerated provided they:

* Remain reasonable
* Do not target individuals
* Do not make members uncomfortable
* Do not violate any other rule

If common sense says it shouldn't be posted, don't post it.

---

# Rule 5 — Gore & Disturbing Content

Graphic, disturbing, and shock content is prohibited.

This includes:

* Gore
* Graphic injuries
* Torture content
* Self-harm content
* Animal abuse content
* Shock videos
* Extremely disturbing media

If content is likely to disturb the average member, it should not be shared.

---

# Rule 6 — Privacy & Personal Information

Members must respect the privacy of others.

The following are prohibited:

* Doxxing
* Sharing personal information without consent
* Publishing private details
* Revealing confidential information
* Sharing sensitive information obtained without permission

Privacy applies both inside and outside TSB communities.

Respect people's boundaries.

---

# Rule 7 — Direct Messages (DMs), Consent & Boundaries

Members are free to communicate privately.

However:

* Harassment is prohibited
* Repeated unwanted messages are prohibited
* Manipulation is prohibited
* Blackmail is prohibited
* Extortion is prohibited
* Stalking is prohibited
* Impersonation is prohibited

If someone asks you to stop contacting them, respect their decision.

"No" means "No."

The Council may review serious DM-related complaints when sufficient evidence is provided.

---

# Rule 8 — Minor Safety

Members must maintain appropriate boundaries with individuals under 18 years of age.

The following are strictly prohibited:

* Grooming
* Exploitation
* Manipulation
* Sexual interactions involving minors
* Soliciting personal information from minors
* Pressuring minors to share photographs
* Pressuring minors to share personal details

Adults are expected to behave responsibly when interacting with younger members.

Violations may result in immediate removal and additional action where appropriate.

---

# Rule 9 — Promotions & Advertising

Members may share one promotional post per week related to:

* Their own project
* Their own business
* Their own content
* Their own service
* Their own community

The following are prohibited:

* Promotion flooding
* Referral spam
* Repeated advertisements
* Misleading promotions
* Unauthorized advertising campaigns

Members seeking additional promotional opportunities may use official TSB promotional systems where available.

The Council reserves the right to remove promotions considered harmful, misleading, deceptive, inappropriate, or disruptive.

---

# Rule 10 — Scams, Fraud & Deception

The following are strictly prohibited:

* Scams
* Fraud
* Phishing
* Fake giveaways
* Fake investments
* Fake services
* Financial deception
* Misleading schemes

Immediate moderation action may be taken against offenders.

Severe violations may result in removal without prior warning.

---

# Rule 11 — Usernames, Profiles & Identity

Members must not intentionally misrepresent themselves.

The following are prohibited:

* Impersonating administrators
* Impersonating moderators
* Impersonating Council members
* Impersonating bots
* Impersonating other members
* NSFW profile pictures
* Scam-related profiles
* Graphic profile pictures
* Misleading identities

Invisible names, moderation-evasion profiles, and deceptive identities may be restricted.

Members may be requested to modify profiles that negatively impact the community.

---

# Rule 12 — Voice Chat Conduct

Voice Chats should remain enjoyable and respectful for all participants.

The following are prohibited:

* Mic spam
* Soundboard abuse
* Ear-damaging audio
* Intentional disruptions
* Voice trolling that negatively impacts the VC experience

Music is permitted.

If the majority of active VC participants wish to continue listening to music, no administrator or moderator may stop, pause, skip, or otherwise interfere with the music solely based on personal preference.

Moderators may only intervene when:

* Rules are violated
* Content becomes disruptive
* Safety concerns arise

Political and religious discussions require designated VCs.

Unauthorized VC recordings are prohibited.

VC recordings may only be made by administrators authorized by the Owner.

---

# Rule 13 — Politics & Religion

Political and religious discussions are not permitted in normal chat.

Such discussions may only occur within Voice Chats specifically designated for those topics.

Participants must remain respectful.

The following remain prohibited:

* Hate speech
* Religious attacks
* Political extremism
* Calls for violence
* Discrimination
* Harassment
* Incitement

Healthy discussion is welcome.

Hostility is not.

---

# Rule 14 — Raids, Brigading & External Conflicts

Members may not:

* Organize raids
* Participate in brigading
* Conduct mass-report campaigns
* Coordinate harassment campaigns
* Bring external community wars into TSB communities

TSB communities must not be used as a base for attacks against individuals, organizations, or communities.

---

# Rule 15 — Council Authority & Community Protection

Not every possible situation can be listed within a written rulebook.

The TSB Council reserves the right to take reasonable and proportionate action against conduct that harms:

* Community members
* Community operations
* Community safety
* Community reputation
* Community stability
* Official TSB services and projects

This authority may be exercised even when specific conduct is not explicitly listed within these rules.

---

# Additional Policies

## Relationship & Flirting Policy

Members are free to form friendships and relationships.

However:

* Relationship drama should not be brought into community chats.
* Romantic conversations should preferably move to DMs.
* Consensual flirting may occur in VCs provided all involved parties are comfortable.
* Harassment and unwanted advances remain prohibited.

---

## Anime & Fandom Policy

Anime, manga, fandom content, edits, and related discussions are allowed.

However:

* Do not flood chats with fandom content.
* Do not repeatedly spam niche content.
* If someone specifically requests anime-related content, sharing through DMs is recommended.

The goal is to keep conversations balanced and enjoyable for everyone.

---

# Final Reminder

Rules exist to protect the community, not to restrict it.

Agar aap common sense use karte ho, dusron ki respect karte ho, boundaries maintain karte ho, aur community ko positively contribute karte ho, then you'll rarely have any issues with these rules.

Help us build a stronger, safer, and better TSB for everyone.
