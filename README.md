# 📖 About

Welcome to the official governance repository of **The Sovereign Brotherhood (TSB)**.

This repository contains the constitutional framework, community rules, staff policies, and dispute resolution procedures governing communities operating under the authority of the **TSB Council**.

The purpose of these documents is to provide:

* Consistent governance
* Community protection
* Fair moderation
* Transparent procedures
* Long-term organizational stability

---

# ⚡ Quick Summary

Before participating in any TSB community:

✅ Respect all members
✅ No harassment or bullying
✅ No NSFW media
✅ No scams or deceptive activities
✅ Respect privacy and consent
✅ Respect age-related boundaries
✅ Follow Voice Chat rules
✅ Follow moderator instructions

# 🏛 Governance Structure

<div align="center">

<img src="https://res.cloudinary.com/dwadwpalt/image/upload/v1780180767/Verification_igqxnr.png" width="250">

TSB operates under a structured governance model designed to maintain fairness, accountability, and long-term community growth.

### Authority Hierarchy

```text
Founder
   ↓
TSB Council
   ↓
Administrators
   ↓
Moderators
   ↓
Members
```

The TSB Council serves as the highest governing authority within communities operating under TSB.
